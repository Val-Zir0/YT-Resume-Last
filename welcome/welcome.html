YT Resume Last

Welcome to the Future of YouTube!

Never lose your place again. Seamlessly resume your videos and music exactly where you left off, with full control over volume, speed, and playlists.

🚀

Features

  • Auto-resume videos & music
  • Restore exact timestamp
  • Restore playback speed & volume
  • Restore playlists & shuffle/repeat
  • Granular control for every feature
💡

How to Use

1. Watch any video on YouTube or listen to a track on YouTube Music.

2. Close the tab. Your session is automatically saved!

3. Open YouTube again, and the extension will magically redirect you to where you left off.
